DIY Classic Strawberry Banana

Published: August 23rd, 2017 by Brooklyn Estremera

Fan of Berry Electric's Classic Strawberry Banana smoothie? Well, today's your lucky day. Continue reading to learn the secrets of concoting this refreshing treat.

Berry Electric's Classic Strawberry Banana Smoothie is a fruit smoothie sent from the heavens. Packed with antioxidants, this delicious cup of fruit packs a punch that will keep you energized for your next project!

Tips before you get started

  • Always wash your hands before touching food
  • Remember to wash your fruits as well! This is crucial in removing any icky bacteria that may have followed the fruits into your home! Stop them in their tracks before they party it up in your tummy.
  • To ensure smoothness of your drink, it's best to make sure each ingredient is completely blended before adding the next. Add liquid to the blender first!

    What You'll Need:

  • A butter knife
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 whole fresh strawberry
  • 1 cup unsweetened milk (nut, soy, animal)
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • Honey or maple syrup, to taste

    How to Make the Magic Happen:

  • Add unsweetened milk to blender
  • Cut banana into 3 sections, add to blender
  • Add ½ cup frozen strawberries to blender
  • Add ½ cup plain Greek yogurt to blender
  • (OPTIONAL) Add honey or maple syrup for a sweet kick
  • Blend
  • Cut 1 whole strawberry into 4 pieces using the butter knife
  • Pour into tall glass, arrange cut strawberry on top
  • Enjoy!
